📋 STNDR · Program Guide

STNDR — Designed by CBUM

4 programs · German Volume · Tri-Sets · Drop AMRAP · Specific Tempo

Program Philosophy
Designer: CBUM (Chris Bumstead) — built around the training methods that shaped a multi-time Classic Physique Olympia champion.
Rest Periods: Longer than most programs — 60 to 180 seconds between sets. Quality over speed.
Tempo: Specific ratios are recommended throughout — they're not optional if you want results. See tempo section below.
Progression: Weight increases as reps decrease across sets. Track your weights each session.
Signature Set Types
⚡ 10×10German Volume Training — 10 sets of 10 reps. High volume, moderate weight, 60 sec rest.
🔺 Tri-Set3 exercises back to back — 10 sec rest between movements, 120-180 sec after the trio.
⚡ Superset2 exercises paired — complete A then B with minimal rest, full rest after the pair.
↘️ Drop AMRAPDrop weight immediately, push to failure. Common finish for isolation movements.
🔢 Cluster SetReps broken into mini-clusters with 10-15 sec intra-set rest. Extends time under tension.
📈 PyramidWeight increases as reps decrease — 12, 10, 8, 6 or 15, 12, 10, 8, 6.

Tempo Ratios
⏱️How to Read Tempo
Format is Eccentric : Pause at bottom : Concentric : Pause at top

3:1:1:0 — 3 sec lower, 1 sec pause, 1 sec lift, no pause at top
4:0:0:0 — 4 sec lower, no pauses, explosive lift
3:3:1:0 — 3 sec lower, 3 sec pause at bottom, 1 sec lift
2:0:1:2 — 2 sec lower, no pause, 1 sec lift, 2 sec squeeze at top
3:0:3:0 — 3 sec lower, explosive lift back up (3 sec)
2:2:1:0 — 2 sec lower, 2 sec pause, 1 sec lift
Programs
📅4 Weeks to Open
Full body splits across 4 weeks with progressive overload. Back, legs, chest/arms, shoulders across days. Tri-sets and supersets are the signature format.
🔄Push / Pull / Legs
Classic PPL rotation with CBUM's volume approach. 10×10 German Volume used heavily on primary compounds. Tri-sets on isolation work.
🏛️Legacy Prep
Competition-style prep program. Higher rep ranges, drop AMRAP finishers on most exercises, 45-60 sec rest periods. Built for conditioning.
💪Bro Split
Traditional body part split — one muscle group per day. Moderate volume, hypertrophy rep ranges, standard CBUM exercise selection.
