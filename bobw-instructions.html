Best of Both Worlds

3 on / 1 off · 4-week block · one body part per day · strength + conditioning

✍️ Designed by Mike Cross
How It Works
Split: One body part per day on a 3-on / 1-off rolling cycle — Legs · Chest · Back · rest · Biceps · Triceps · Shoulders · rest (an 8-day cycle).
Each session: exactly 4 lifts, plus two cardio pieces (1 LISS + 1 HIIT).
Progression: the exercises stay the same every week — what changes week to week is the rep scheme & intensity style. Run the same 4-week block and the stimulus rotates underneath you.
The 4 Exercise Slots
1 · Pyramid
Progressive — weight up, reps down across sets.
2 · Heavy
Strength focus, low rep range.
3 · Tempo
Controlled eccentric / pauses.
4 · High Volume
Endurance / pump, higher reps.

Daily Cardio · Two Forms Required
Every training day includes both a LISS and a HIIT piece. Pull a session from the Conditioning Corner.
🚶
LISS — Low-Intensity Steady State
15–20 min
Steady, conversational pace at a low, sustainable intensity. Your pick of modality — incline walk, bike, row, or stair-climber.
⚡
HIIT — High-Intensity Interval Training
10–15 min
Short, hard intervals against easy recoveries (e.g. 30s hard / 30s easy). Push the work bouts near-max, then back off.
