Daily Gainz — Designed by Bradley Martyn

8 programs · 5×5 strength · drop sets · failure · 300-rep ab circuits

Program Philosophy
Designer: Bradley Martyn — built around high-volume, full-week programming with multiple structure options.
Goal Variants: Programs split between Lean Bulk and Shred focus. Same movements, different volume and rest.
Drop Sets: Used heavily on isolation movements — cable crossovers, lat pulldowns, preacher curls. Each drop set goes to failure.
Ab Volume: 300 total reps on ab days — incline sit-ups, hanging leg raises, or knee raises accumulated over the session.

Signature Patterns
5×5Strength anchor on primary lifts — deadlift, squat, bench. Go heavy.
Drop SetsCable crossovers, lat pulldowns, cable rows — strip weight, continue to failure. Standard finishing tool.
3 sec pauseUsed on concentration curls and preacher work. Pauses at peak contraction.
Failure SetsPull-ups and push-ups always go to failure. 3 sets each session.
High Rep IsolationLateral raises, calf raises, front raises — 12-15 or 20-25 reps. Light and controlled.
Programs
🏗️Men's Lean Bulk
5-day split — Back/Bis, Chest/Tris, Legs/Abs, Shoulders/Traps, Arms. 5×5 compounds + 4×8-12 accessory + drop sets on cables. Rest Days 6 & 7.
🔥Men's Shred
5-day split with higher reps on isolation. 3-4 sets vs 4-5. More drop sets. Lower rest. Same compound anchors (5×5). Active rest day option Day 3.
💚Every Chest Day
Chest hit every day — paired with back, shoulders, arms, or legs. Includes chest mobility work (static holds, broomstick stretch) 2x per week.
💪Every Arms Day
Arms hit daily — paired with larger muscle groups. High frequency bicep/tricep volume with 4×8-12 and failure sets on dips and push-ups.
📅5-On / 2-Off · 3-On / 1-Off · 2-On / 1-Off
Frequency variants — same programming structure, different weekly cadence. Choose based on recovery and schedule.
