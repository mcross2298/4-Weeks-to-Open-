📋 Daily Pump · Program Guide

Daily Pump — Designed by Julian Smith

9 workouts · Pyramid · Reverse Pyramid · Drop Sets · Tempo Variations

Program Philosophy
Designer: Julian Smith — built around variation and maximizing muscle pump through different set structures in every session.
Variation Tabs: Each workout has multiple modes — Standard, Drop Sets, Tempo, Pyramid, or Reverse Pyramid. Same exercises, different execution.
Remixed Variations: 🔀 indicates a remixed version with different exercise order, pairing, or rep target.
Rest: Not prescribed — self-regulate based on the intensity of the variation you chose. Drop sets need more rest than standard sets.
Variation Modes
📊 StandardStraight sets — consistent reps across all sets. Foundation of each workout.
📈 Pyramid12, 10, 8, 6, 4 — weight increases as reps decrease. Start moderate, finish heavy.
🔄 Reverse Pyramid12, 10, 8, 6, 6, 8, 10, 12 — go heavy then come back down. Double the volume.
↘️ Drop Sets4×4 drop 6/8 — start at 4 reps heavy, drop weight and push more reps. Multiple drops per set.
⏱️ Tempo1:0:1:2 / 1:2:1:0 / 4:0:1:0 — slow eccentrics and pauses at contraction or stretch.
🔀 RemixedSame exercises, different order or pairing. Keeps the body guessing session to session.
Tempo Guide
⏱️Reading Tempo Ratios
Format: Eccentric : Pause at bottom : Concentric : Pause at top

1:0:1:2 — 1 sec lower, no pause, 1 sec lift, 2 sec squeeze at top
1:2:1:0 — 1 sec lower, 2 sec pause at bottom (stretch), 1 sec lift
4:0:1:0 — 4 sec negative (slow lower), explosive lift
1:5:1:0 — 1 sec lower, 5 sec pause at bottom (deep stretch hold)

Workouts
💪Biceps & Triceps
5 variation modes — standard pyramid (12,10,8,6,4), drop sets (4×4 drop), tempo (1:0:1:2 pause at peak), plus remixed versions. Same 9 exercises, different execution each mode.
🦵Hams & Glutes
Reverse pyramid (12,10,8,6,6,8,10,12) is the signature format. Heavy barbell stiff leg deadlift as 4×4 anchor. Tempo on good mornings (1:2:1:0 — 2 sec pause at bottom). Hip thrust staple across all variations.
🔙Back & Traps
Lat pulldown, cable rows, DB rows as foundation. Drop AMRAP on pulling movements. Straight arm pulldowns as finisher. Barbell shrug with tempo pause.
💥Chest & Triceps
Incline, flat, and decline variations per tab. Drop AMRAP on cable/pec deck work. JM press and skull crushers with specific tempo on tricep variations.
🏔️Shoulders / Back / Forearms
High rep lateral raises (3×30 in tri-set format). Barbell upright row pyramid. Forearm curls 4×20 finisher. Multiple delt angles across variations.
🦵Legs
Quad extensions as high-rep warmup (3×20-25). Squat or hack squat as low-rep anchor (5×5-8). Leg press pyramid. High rep calf work (5×12-15).
