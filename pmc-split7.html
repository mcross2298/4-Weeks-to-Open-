Split #7 — Giant Sets

4 body parts · 3–5 rounds each · Electric Orange

How Giant Sets Work: Complete every exercise in the list back to back with no rest between exercises. When all exercises are done = 1 Round. Rest 2–3 min. Repeat for 3–5 rounds total. Week 1 and Week 2 use the same exercises — aim for more rounds or heavier weight in W2.
