Best of Both Worlds

A strict, high-performance regime balancing heavy resistance training with cardiovascular conditioning. 3 on / 1 off · 4-week block.

✍️ Designed by Mike Cross
📋 Program Guide & Week-by-Week
✅ Full 4-Week Block
6 body parts · 4 lifts each · daily cardio
A 3-on / 1-off rolling split across a 4-week block, 1 body part per day, exactly 4 lifts per session (Pyramid · Heavy · Tempo · High Volume), plus daily cardio (1 LISS + 1 HIIT) via the Conditioning Corner. Week 1 mixed protocol · Week 2 all-heavy strength · Week 3 all-tempo · Week 4 all-pyramid.
